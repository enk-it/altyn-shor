About Sheregesh

A mountain resort for active travel

Sheregesh has 23 lifts, 38 ski runs and more than 150 hotels and apart-hotels. Annual tourist flow is already about 1.7 million people and may reach 3 million by 2030.

About Altyn-Shor

A single resort accommodation cluster

The project includes an apart-hotel, 4-star and 5-star hotels, a sanatorium with a medical clinic and private chalets. The figure 38 refers to Sheregesh ski runs, not to hotels in the complex.

Project logic

Accommodation, wellness and leisure in one plan

The concept combines lodging, ski infrastructure, wellness facilities, culture, walking areas and summer recreation. The total room stock is 5,000 rooms.

Getting there

Map and route

Altyn-Shor is located in sector F of Sheregesh on Mount Zelenaya. A transport hub near Shoria-Grad and a feeder lift to sector F operate here.

By car: access to sector F from Sheregesh.

Airport: 7 km from Sheregesh.

Transport hub: transfer via the station square.
